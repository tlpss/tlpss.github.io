### Courses
I am a teaching assistent for the course on Microcontrollers in the bachelor of computer science engineering at Ghent University. In this project-based course students learn about the fundamentals of microcontrollers by programming a 2D plotter, learning a robot to crawl or developing a game using gyroscopes and infrared communication. 


### Master Students
I co-advise several master students on their thesis project on diverse topics related to robotic manipulation.
