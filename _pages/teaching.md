### Courses
I am a teaching assistent for the course on Microcontrollers in the bachelor of computer science engineering at Ghet University. In this project-based course students learn about the fundamentals of microcontrollers by programming a 2D plotter, learning a robot to crawl or developing  a game using Gyroscopes and Infrared Communication.
